Bookmarklets

To install, drag the links not titled 'src' to your toolbar.

  • Instagram Sizes (src) - Shows the various sized images for an instagram image. To use: (1) Click on a thumbnail (2) Run this (3) See the output in the dev console.
  • Twitter Like All (src) - Likes all the tweets on a twitter page, then scrolls to the bottom so you can resume.
  • Wikipedia Menu Hover (src) - Makes wikipedia menus hover and track with the page, e.g. https://imgur.com/a/N6kMzrg.
